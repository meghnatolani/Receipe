Biscuit Cake


Making irresistible, original recipe foods with Aloha Spirit since 1950.Here is the famous Biscuit Cake.

INGREDIENTS
  1. 1 12-pack Sweet Dinner Rolls
  2. 1 1/2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  3. 1 1/2 sticks Butter
  4. Capsicum /Bell pepper - 1 cup
  5. Salt and pepper powder to taste
  6. Oregano powder
DIRECTIONS
  1. Melt butter and mix in mustard, sauce and onion.
  2. Cut the entire pack of rolls in half, horizontally (keeping all top and bottom halves separately in tact).
  3. Add broccoli, capsicum and cook for a few minutes in high flame.Keep stirring it continuously. Do not over cook.The vegetables should be crisp.
  4. Then sprinkle a little oregano, add salt and pepper powder to taste.

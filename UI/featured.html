Vegetarian Pasta


Farfalle are a type of pasta. They are commonly known as "bow-tie pasta".The name is derived from the Italian word farfalla which means "butterfly".Today we will learn how to make a very easy Vegetarian Pasta recipe.

INGREDIENTS
  1. Onion -1
  2. Garlic - 1 tbsp
  3. Broccoli - 10-12 bite size florets
  4. Capsicum /Bell pepper - 1 cup
  5. Salt and pepper powder to taste
  6. Oregano powder
Steps
  1. Put broccoli in boiling water for 3-4 minutes.Drain the water and keep it aside.
  2. Heat olive oil, add finely chopped onion and garlic and saute till onions become light pink.
  3. Add broccoli, capsicum and cook for a few minutes in high flame.Keep stirring it continuously. Do not over cook.The vegetables should be crisp.
  4. Then sprinkle a little oregano, add salt and pepper powder to taste.
